Private Nam Khan Riverside Villa Near Luang Prabang Old Town

Villa Visoun is a traditional Lao teak house fully renovated for a peaceful private stay on the Nam Khan River. This entire-villa rental welcomes up to 4 guests with 2 bedrooms, 1 king bed, 2 single beds, 2 baths, a private infinity pool, outdoor jacuzzi, sauna, covered car park, fully equipped kitchen, high-speed Wi-Fi, free airport transfer, and easy access to Luang Prabang old town.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Villa Visoun located?

Villa Visoun is located on the Nam Khan riverside in Luang Prabang, Laos, near the UNESCO old town area.

Does Villa Visoun have a private pool?

Yes. The villa has a private infinity pool, plus an outdoor jacuzzi and sauna.

Is airport transfer included?

Yes. Free airport transfer is included, and train station transfer can be arranged on request.

How do I book Villa Visoun?

You can book through the official Airbnb listing or message Villa Visoun directly on WhatsApp.

What are the check-in and checkout times?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM to 8:00 PM, and checkout is before 11:00 AM.
